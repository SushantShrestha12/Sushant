Q NO.1       Scarcity is the central problem of economy. Justify this statement.

Ans:-      Human wants are unlimited, but the resources are limited and those resources are supplied in less amount than the demand. It is known as Scarcity.

Scarcity is the central problem of the economy because while resources are limited, we are living in a society with unlimited wants. Therefore we have to choose. We have to make trade-offs. We have to effectively allocate resources. We have to do these things because resources are limited and cannot meet out own unlimited demands. People are creating unlimited demands, but there is less supply than the demand so, that's why scarcity is the central problem of economy. People have choice to choose the resources which are avaliable.

In conclusion, Scarcity is the main problem of economy because we have to choose the limited resources over the unlimited wants.

Production Possibility Curve

Introduction

Ans:-       Human wants are unlimited but resources are available to fulfill those wants are limited, they have alternative uses. The production possibility curve is also known as the production frontier curve or the production boundary curve to the production transformation curve.

Statement

Production possibility curve refers to locus focus of various alternative combinations of two commodities that can be produced utilizing the available means and resources and given technology by an economy in a certain time period

Assumptions

  • The economy is producing only two goods.
  • There is full employment of resources.
  • Production technology is given and constant.
  • The factor of production is given and constant.
  • The time period is given.

    Shift in PPC curve

    Ans:-       The movement of the entire PPC form from its initial position to inward or outward position, due to the various reasons is called a shift in the PPC curve.

    Rightward shift in PPC curve

    Ans:-       If the PPC curve moves upward due to technological progress, discover natural resources, increase in the productivity of human resources, increase in the capital investment and increase in the capital formation, increase in the size of the population, etc is known as a rightward shift in PPC curve.

    Reasons for rightward shift in PPC curve

  • Due to technological change.
  • Due to discoveries of natural resources in larger quantities.
  • Due to the increase in the productivity of labor.
  • Due to the increase in investment in capital and the increase in capital formation.
  • Due to the increase in the size of the population.

    • Leftward shift in PPC curve

    Ans:-       If the PPC curve moves backward or inward due to the backwardness in technology, shortage of natural resources, lack of capital investment, lack of machinery, lack of skilled human resources, decrease in size of the population, migration, etc is known as a leftward shift in PPC curve.

    Reasons for leftward shift in PPC curve

  • Due to the backwardness in technology.
  • Due to the shortage of natural resources
  • Due to the lack of training for the labor.
  • Due to the decrease in capital investment.
  • Due to the decrease in the size of the population.
