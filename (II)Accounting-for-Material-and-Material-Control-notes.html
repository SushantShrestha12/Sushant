Short answer questions

1. Considered the following information:
Lead time    2-4 days
Daily usage    400-600 units
Required: (a) Average or normal lead time
(b) Average usage or consumption
(c) Reorder level.

(a) Average lead time

(2+4)/2
= 3 days.

(b) Average usage

(400 + 600)/2
= 500 units.

(c) Reorder level

max usage x max lead time
= (600 x 4) units
= 2400 units.

2. You are given the following information:
Delivery period    4-6 days
Weekly usage    120-240 units
Days in week    6 days
Required: (a) Daily minimum usage
(b) Daily maximum usage
(c) Average daily usage
(d) Average lead time
(e) Reorder level.

(a) Daily minimum usage

120/6
= 20 units.

(b) Daily maximum usage

240/6
= 40 units.

(c) Average daily usage

(20 + 40)/2
= 60/2
= 30 units.

(d) Average lead time

(4+6)/6
= 5 days.

(e) Reorder level

max usage x max lead time
= (40 x 6) units
= 240 units.

3. A Company has furnished the following details:
Lead time    2-4 weeks
Weekly usage    3000-7000 units
Required: (a) Reordering point
(b) Minimum stock level or safety stock.

(a) Reordering point

Max lead time x Max usage
= 4 x 7000
= 28000 units.

(b) Maximum stock level

Rol - (Normal usage x Normal lead time)
= 28000 - (5000 x 3)
= 13000 units.
