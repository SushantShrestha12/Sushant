1. Considered the following information:
Lead time    2-4 days
Daily usage    400-600 units
Required: (a) Average or normal lead time
(b) Average usage or consumption
(c) Reorder level.

Solution:
(a) Average lead time

2+4 2
= 3 days.

(b) Average usage

(400 + 600 ) 2
= 500 units.

(c) Reorder level

max usage x max lead time
= (600 x 4) units
= 2400 units.

(b) Daily maximum usage

(e) Reorder level.

3. A Company has furnished the following details:
Lead time    2-4 weeks
Weekly usage    3000-7000 units
Required: (a) Reordering point
(b) Minimum stock level or safety stock.

Solution:
(a) Reordering point

Max lead time x Max usage
= 4 x 7000
= 28000 units.

(b) Maximum stock level

ROL - (Normal usage x Normal lead time)
= 28000 - (5000 x 3)
= 13000 units.

5. XYZ Company provides the following information:
Reorder period    4-8 days
Reorder quantity    7000 units
Material consumed per day    500 to 1500 units

(b) Minimum stock level
(c) Maximum stock level
(d) Average stock level

Solution:
(a) Reorder level

Max lead time x max usage
= 8 x 1500
= 12000 units.

(b) Minimum stock level

ROL - (Normal usage x Normal lead time)
= 12000 - (1000 x 6)
= 6000 units.

(c) Maximum stock level

(ROL + ROQ) - (min usage x min lead time)
= (12000 + 7000) - (500 x 4)
= 19000-2000
= 17000 units.

(d) Average stock level

Min stock level + ½ ROQ
6000 + ½ x 7000
= 9500 units.
