Ans:- Critical thinking is about judgment over whatever people have already done. Everyone thinks but not everyone thinks critically.

According to Josh Lanyon, Not everyone has the ability to critically thinking but everyone should have it as a very important skill. In day-to-day life, people do their jobs, study, and do their regular stuff but they do not take the time for critical thinking they only do their important thing not knowing that critical thinking is also important to them.
