a. What is Hyperloop? How does it work?

A hyperloop is an ultra-high speed ground transportation system for passenger and cargo. It work by 'floating' pod which shoots through giant, low-pressure tubes either above or below ground.

In Nepal, the number of private vehicle is expanding on a daily basis, to the point where there are more private vehicles than intented or necessary. People in the middle class and upper class are buying automobiles, which increases the number of private vehicles. Private vehicles not only have an impact on environment, but they may also have an impact on our health, causing diseases such as cancer, lungs problem, and difficulty in breathing. There are so many private automobiles that there is no space for a footpath. Since, people are parking their cars on the sidewalk.

Private automobiles are more expensive than public vehicles to the general public, their usage should be reduced as much as possible. Instead of utilizing private vehicles, we should utilize public vehicles, which are also less expansive. Electric vehicles are another option for both private and public transportation. Electric vehicles are powered by electricity rather than gasoline or diesal. Because an electric car is powered by electricity, there is no smoke or polluntants to harm our health or the environment. Electric automobiles, such as Tesla, are prohibitively expensive for the middle class. As a result, it is preferable to use public transportation rather than a private automobile or an electric cars.

So, In order to reduce the ever-increasing number of private automobiles, using public transportation is the greatest option for us, the environment, animals, and so on.
