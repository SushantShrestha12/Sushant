Rakesh's success was a topic of conversation in the neighborhood because he was the family's first son to get a higher education and pass the Medical Exam.

The author makes fun of the terms 'America' and 'the USA' by saying that Rakesh's father is unaware that both America and the USA are similar, thus he taught everyone in the home to say America but not the USA.

Rakesh's wife is characterized by the book as an old-fashioned, ignorant, fat woman. She was lazy, placid, and complaisant, but she was too nice to even try to force Rakesh to leave the house and sit up on his own.

Rakesh began his work in the city hospital, quickly climbing to the top of the administrative hierarchy and becoming a director before leaving to open his own clinic.
