Share capital of company

Short answer question

Shree Co. Ltd. issued 10000 equity shares of Rs. 100 each for public subscription. Amount was payable as Rs 30 on application, Rs 40 on allotment, Rs 30 on first and final call. All the shares were subscribed and fully paid.
Required: Journal entries.


Particulars L.F Debit Credit

On application

Bank A/c.......Dr
    To Share application A/c
(Being share application money received) 		300000 300000
Share Application A/c.......Dr
    To Share capital A/c
(Being share application money transferred to share capital account.) 		300000 300000

On allotment

Share allotment A/c.......Dr
    To Share capital A/c
(Being share allotment money due) 		400000 400000
Bank A/c.......Dr
    To Share allotment A/c
(Being share allotment money received.) 		400000 400000

On F/F call

Share F/F call A/c.......Dr
    To Share capital A/c
(Being share F/F call money made due) 		300000 300000
Bank A/c.......Dr
    To Share F/F call A/c
(Being share F/F call money received.) 		300000 300000
